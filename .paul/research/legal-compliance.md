# Legal & Compliance Research — Lipa Health

## Classification
Position as: **educational wellness platform**, NOT medical device.
Key language: "prevention and health education insights" (Lucis model)

## Entity Structure
- Phase 1: US LLC + EU Representative (EUR 2-5K/yr) + UK Representative (EUR 1-3K/yr)
- Phase 2: EU subsidiary in Ireland (10% tax) or Estonia (0% reinvested)

## Insurance Required
- Tech E&O + Cyber: $5-15K/yr (must affirmatively cover AI outputs)
- Product liability: recommended if affiliate links to supplements
- Provider: Admiral Insurance "Virtual Care" or Vouch (health tech specialist)

## Peptide Risk
- HIGH RISK to personalize peptide recommendations based on blood results
- Launch approach: educational content only, no personalized recs, no vendor links for peptides
- Phase 2: add with medical advisor review + legal counsel approval

## Country Risk Ranking
1. Spain — MEDIUM-LOW (regulatory gap, incoming Digital Health Law 2027-2028)
2. Poland — MEDIUM (developing enforcement)
3. Netherlands — MEDIUM-HIGH (hierarchy principle: if in doubt, it's a medicine)
4. UK — MEDIUM-HIGH (established framework, CQC for medical activities)
5. Germany — HIGH (strictest in EU, launch last)

## Critical Safety Feature
Must build: critical value alert system. Flag dangerous levels, force user acknowledgment, recommend seeking medical attention. Legally protective + ethically required.

## Required Disclaimers (ToS)
1. Not medical device / not medical advice / not diagnosis
2. No doctor-patient relationship created
3. AI may produce inaccurate outputs
4. User responsible for verifying + consulting healthcare professional
5. User assumes risk + indemnifies platform
6. Liability capped at 12-month subscription amount
7. Affiliate relationships disclosed (EU UCPD requirement)

## Pre-Launch Legal Costs
- EU/UK Representatives: EUR 3-8K/yr
- ToS legal review: EUR 5-15K one-time
- Insurance: $5-15K/yr
- Medical advisor: equity + small retainer
- Total: ~EUR 15-30K first year
