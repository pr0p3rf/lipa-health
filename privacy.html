Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Lipa Health is operated by Go Exe B.V., registered in Amsterdam, Netherlands. We take the privacy of your health data seriously. This policy explains exactly what we collect, why, and what we do with it — in plain language.

What data we collect

When you use Lipa, we collect:

  • Account information — your email address and basic profile details (age, sex) that you provide during signup. These help us personalize your analysis.
  • Blood test documents — the PDFs or images you upload for analysis. We extract biomarker names and values from these documents.
  • Biomarker data — the structured values we extract from your tests (e.g. "Vitamin D: 62 nmol/L"). This is stored separately from your original files for faster analysis.
  • Usage data — basic information about how you use the platform (pages visited, features used). We do not use third-party analytics trackers.

How we use your data

Your data is used for one purpose: giving you a useful, personalized analysis of your blood work. Specifically:

  • Analysis — we process your biomarker values to generate your health report, including trends over time if you upload multiple tests.
  • Personalization — your age and sex are used to apply the correct reference ranges and generate relevant recommendations.
  • Product improvement — we use aggregated, anonymized usage patterns to improve the product. We never use your individual health data for this purpose.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal or health data with third parties for their own purposes. Ever.

Third-party services

To provide the analysis, we use a small number of trusted services. Here is exactly who touches your data and why:

  • Anthropic (Claude) — we send your biomarker values to Claude for AI-powered analysis and report generation. Anthropic does not use API inputs to train their models. Your data is processed and not retained beyond the API call.
  • OpenAI — we use OpenAI's embedding models to match your biomarkers against our research database. Only biomarker names and values are sent, not your identity. OpenAI does not use API inputs for training.
  • Supabase — our database and authentication provider. All data is stored on Supabase infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit.
  • Stripe — handles payment processing. We never see or store your full credit card number. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment data.

That is the complete list. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook pixels, or any advertising trackers.

Data storage and security

Your data is stored in Supabase (hosted on AWS infrastructure in the EU). All data is encrypted at rest and all connections use TLS encryption in transit. Access to production data is restricted and logged.

Your uploaded PDFs are stored in encrypted object storage. Extracted biomarker values are stored in an encrypted database. Authentication uses secure, hashed tokens.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies — only what is needed for authentication. Specifically, we store a session token so you stay logged in. That is it. No tracking cookies, no third-party cookies, no cookie banner needed because we are not doing anything shady with cookies.

Your rights

You have full control over your data. At any time, you can:

  • Access — view all the data we hold about you directly in your dashboard.
  • Export — download your complete data, including all biomarker values and reports.
  • Delete — permanently delete any individual test, or delete your entire account and all associated data. When you delete, we delete. No 90-day retention, no backups we conveniently forget about.
  • Correct — update your profile information at any time.

If you are in the EU, these rights are guaranteed under the GDPR. But we offer them to everyone regardless of where you live.

Data retention

We keep your data for as long as you have an account. If you delete a test, it is gone immediately. If you delete your account, all your data — profile, tests, biomarkers, reports — is permanently deleted within 48 hours.

We do not keep "anonymized" copies of your health data after deletion.

Children

Lipa is not designed for or directed at anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

Changes to this policy

If we make meaningful changes to this policy, we will notify you by email before the changes take effect. We will not quietly reduce your privacy protections.

Questions about your privacy? Contact us at privacy@lipa.health.
