Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

Lipa Health ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we protect it.

What we collect

When you join our waitlist, we collect:

  • Your email address
  • The date you signed up

We do not collect health data, payment information, or any other personal data at this stage.

Why we collect it

To notify you when Lipa Health launches and to send you relevant updates about our product. That's it.

How we store it

Your email is stored securely in a database hosted within the European Union (Supabase, EU region). Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

Who we share it with

Nobody. We do not sell, rent, or share your email with any third party.

Cookies & tracking

Our waitlist page does not use cookies. If we run advertising campaigns, ad platforms (such as Meta) may use their own tracking pixels. These are governed by the respective platform's privacy policy.

Your rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@lipa.health.

Changes

We may update this policy as our product develops. Any changes will be posted on this page.
