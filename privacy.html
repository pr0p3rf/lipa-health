Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Lipa respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and your rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Lipa is a trade name of Go Exe B.V., a private limited company registered in the Netherlands. Go Exe B.V. is the data controller for the purposes of this policy.

1. What data we collect

Waitlist signup

When you join our waitlist, we collect:

  • Your email address
  • The date and time you signed up
  • (Optional) The specific compound or research area you expressed interest in

Order and account data

When you create an account or place an order, we collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Shipping and billing address
  • Phone number (for delivery purposes)
  • Order history
  • Payment method confirmation (we do NOT store full card details — these are handled by our PCI-compliant payment processor)
  • A declaration that you are at least 18 years of age and are purchasing for research purposes

Website usage data

When you visit the Lipa website, we may collect:

  • Basic analytics data (pages viewed, time on site, referring source)
  • Device type, browser, and approximate location (country level)
  • IP address (processed transiently for security and fraud prevention)

We use privacy-focused analytics and do not track individuals across other websites.

2. Legal basis for processing (GDPR Art. 6)

We process your personal data under one or more of the following legal bases:

  • Contract performance — processing is necessary to fulfill your order and deliver products you purchased
  • Legitimate interest — for fraud prevention, service improvement, and operational security
  • Consent — for marketing emails and newsletter subscriptions (you can withdraw consent at any time)
  • Legal obligation — for tax, accounting, and regulatory record-keeping

3. How we use your data

We use personal data only for the following purposes:

  • Processing and shipping your orders
  • Notifying you about order status, shipping, and delivery
  • Providing customer support and responding to inquiries
  • Sending newsletter and research updates (only if you opted in)
  • Fraud prevention and security
  • Compliance with tax and legal obligations
  • Improving the website and our services

4. Who we share your data with

We share personal data only with trusted third parties who help us operate Lipa. We never sell your data.

  • Payment processors (to process your payment securely)
  • Shipping carriers (PostNL, DHL) to deliver your orders
  • Third-party fulfillment provider (EU-based, for order pick/pack/ship)
  • Hosting provider (Supabase, EU region) for data storage
  • Email service provider for transactional and marketing emails
  • Tax and accounting partners where required by law

All processors are GDPR-compliant and bound by data processing agreements.

5. How long we keep your data

  • Waitlist signups: Until you request deletion or unsubscribe
  • Order records: 7 years (for Dutch tax and accounting law compliance)
  • Account data: For the duration of your account, plus 7 years after closure for tax compliance
  • Marketing consents: Until you withdraw consent
  • Analytics data: 14 months

6. Your rights under GDPR

As a data subject under EU law, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten"): Request deletion of your data, subject to legal retention requirements
  • Right to restrict processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to object: Object to certain types of processing (including direct marketing)
  • Right to withdraw consent: Withdraw marketing consent at any time
  • Right to lodge a complaint: File a complaint with the Dutch Data Protection Authority (Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens) at autoriteitpersoonsgegevens.nl

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@lipa.bio. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Data security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data, including:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS/HTTPS) and at rest
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Data storage within the European Union
  • Regular security audits of our systems
  • Vendor agreements with all processors

While we take reasonable measures to protect your data, no system is 100% secure. If we become aware of a data breach involving your personal data, we will notify you and the relevant authorities as required by GDPR (within 72 hours for serious breaches).

8. Cookies

We use cookies only where necessary for the website to function and for basic analytics. You will be presented with a cookie consent banner on your first visit, allowing you to accept or reject non-essential cookies. You can change your cookie preferences at any time via the cookie settings link in the footer.

Categories of cookies we may use:

  • Essential: Required for the site to function (session, cart, security) — no consent needed
  • Analytics: Aggregated, anonymous usage data — requires consent
  • Marketing: We do not use third-party marketing cookies at this time

9. International transfers

Your data is primarily processed within the European Union. If any processor is located outside the EU, we ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place (such as Standard Contractual Clauses) as required by GDPR.

10. Children

Lipa products and services are not intended for anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a minor, please contact us immediately for deletion.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted on this page with the "Last updated" date revised. For significant changes, we will notify waitlist and account holders by email.
