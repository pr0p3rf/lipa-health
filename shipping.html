Shipping & Delivery

Last updated: April 2026

Lipa ships from our fulfillment center in the Netherlands to addresses across the European Union and the United Kingdom. All orders are shipped via PostNL and DHL Express. Because we ship from within the EU, intra-EU orders do not face customs clearance delays.

The table below shows estimated delivery times from order confirmation to delivery. Times are business days and exclude order processing time (1 business day typically).

Shipping costs

Shipping is calculated at checkout based on destination and order weight. Free shipping is available for orders above a threshold (to be announced at launch). Typical ranges:

  • Netherlands: €3.50 - €5.50
  • EU Zone 1 (BE, LU, DE): €5.50 - €8.00
  • EU Zone 2 (FR, ES, IT, PL, etc.): €7.00 - €12.00
  • UK: €8.00 - €14.00 (plus any UK customs/VAT)

Processing time

Orders placed before 14:00 CET on business days are typically processed and handed to the carrier the same day. Orders placed after 14:00 CET or on weekends/holidays are processed the next business day. You will receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information once your order leaves our warehouse.

Packaging

Every Lipa order is shipped in professional laboratory packaging with foam inserts to protect vials during transit. Outer packaging is plain and discretely branded. Your printed Certificate of Analysis is included in every order. Vials are lyophilized (freeze-dried) and shelf-stable at room temperature — no cold chain is required for the compounds we carry.

Customs and duties

For orders within the European Union, no customs declaration is required since Lipa ships from the Netherlands. Orders are not subject to customs clearance or import duties when shipped within the EU single market.

For UK orders (post-Brexit), shipments are subject to UK import VAT and any applicable duties. Lipa handles the customs declaration, but you, as the importer, are responsible for any UK import charges. These are collected by the carrier before delivery.

Countries we ship to

Lipa currently ships to the following destinations:

  • All EU member states
  • United Kingdom
  • Switzerland (by special arrangement)
  • Norway (by special arrangement)

Countries we do NOT ship to

Lipa does not ship to:

  • United States (due to FDA restrictions on research chemicals)
  • Canada, Australia, New Zealand
  • Any country where the import of research chemicals is prohibited or requires special licensing

Tracking

Every order includes full tracking via the carrier's website. You will receive a tracking number and link in your shipping confirmation email. Most EU orders can be tracked from handover through delivery.

Failed delivery

If a delivery attempt fails (recipient not home, incorrect address), the carrier will typically make a second attempt or leave the package at a nearby pickup point. You will receive notification from the carrier with next steps. Please respond promptly to carrier notifications — packages unclaimed at pickup points are returned to our warehouse after 14 days, and reshipment will incur additional shipping costs.

Damaged or lost shipments

If your order arrives damaged or does not arrive within the expected timeframe, please contact us. See our Returns Policy for full details on how we handle damaged or lost shipments.
