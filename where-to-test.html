Frequently asked questions

Common questions

Do I need a doctor's referral?

It depends on your country. In many places (US, UK, Poland, India, Thailand, UAE, Spain, Italy, Brazil) you can self-order blood tests directly. In others (France, Belgium) you need a prescription, but a quick telehealth consultation solves that in minutes. For countries like Canada, Australia, and Germany, telehealth workarounds exist to get referrals affordably.

What markers should I test?

For a comprehensive health overview, request: Complete Blood Count (CBC), Comprehensive Metabolic Panel, Lipid Panel (total cholesterol, LDL, HDL, triglycerides), Thyroid (TSH, FT3, FT4), Vitamin D, Vitamin B12, Iron and Ferritin, and hs-CRP (inflammation). This covers the core markers Lipa analyzes.

How do I get my results as a PDF?

Most modern labs provide results through an online portal or app where you can download a PDF. Some labs also send results via email or WhatsApp. Look for a "Download" or "Export" button in the patient portal. If you only have paper results, take a clear photo or scan them.
