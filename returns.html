Returns & Refunds Policy

Last updated: April 2026

We stand behind the quality of every product Lipa ships. Every compound in our catalog is independently verified by Janoshik Analytical before it reaches our warehouse, and we want you to receive exactly what you ordered, in good condition. This page describes our policy for returns, refunds, and replacements.

Short version: We offer full refunds or replacements for damaged, defective, or incorrect orders. Due to the nature of research chemicals and sterility, we cannot accept returns of opened or tampered products. Claims must be made within 14 days of delivery.

1. Damaged or defective orders

If your order arrives damaged, with broken vials, compromised packaging, or products that appear defective, contact us within 14 days of delivery at hello@lipa.bio. Please include:

  • Your order number
  • Clear photos of the damage (including the outer packaging, inner packaging, and the affected product)
  • A brief description of what is wrong

We will review your claim promptly and offer you a full refund or a free replacement at your choice.

2. Incorrect orders

If we ship you the wrong product (wrong compound, wrong vial size, wrong quantity), we will correct it at no cost to you. Contact us within 14 days of delivery with your order number and a description of the error. We will ship the correct product immediately and arrange return or disposal of the incorrect item.

3. Products not matching their Certificate of Analysis

Every Lipa product ships with an independently verified Certificate of Analysis (COA) from Janoshik Analytical. If you have concerns that a product does not match the published COA, contact us. We reserve the right to request that the customer provide independent third-party test results before issuing a refund, but we will work with you in good faith to resolve any legitimate concern.

4. Products that cannot be returned

Due to the nature of research chemicals, regulatory considerations, and sterility of packaging, the following cannot be accepted for return or refund:

  • Products where the tamper-evident seal has been broken
  • Products where the vial stopper has been punctured
  • Products that have been opened, used, or tampered with in any way
  • Products that have been stored outside of recommended conditions by the purchaser
  • Products for which the customer simply changed their mind (buyer's remorse)
  • Products returned after 14 days from delivery without prior written authorization

5. Change-of-mind returns

We do not accept returns for change-of-mind reasons on any research chemical products. Because these products must be handled, stored, and documented under specific laboratory conditions, we cannot resell any unit that has left our controlled supply chain. We appreciate your understanding.

6. Accessories and laboratory supplies

For laboratory supplies (bacteriostatic water, syringes, preparation kits), we can accept returns of unopened, sealed items within 14 days of delivery. The customer is responsible for return shipping costs. Once we receive and verify that items are in resaleable condition, we will issue a refund to the original payment method within 10 business days.

7. Refunds

Approved refunds are processed to the original payment method within 10 business days of approval. Depending on your payment provider and bank, it may take an additional 3-10 business days for the refund to appear in your account.

8. Lost or undelivered orders

If your tracking indicates that your order was not delivered or appears lost in transit, contact us at hello@lipa.bio with your order number. We will file an investigation with the carrier and, depending on the outcome, offer you a refund or replacement. Please allow up to 14 days for investigation.

9. Consumer rights (EU)

Nothing in this policy is intended to limit or exclude any rights you have under EU consumer protection law (including the Consumer Rights Directive 2011/83/EU) or Dutch consumer law where those rights apply to research-use products. EU consumers ordering from Lipa acknowledge that research chemicals fall into a specific category with different conditions than general consumer goods. Where statutory rights apply, they are preserved.

10. How to contact us

For any returns, refunds, or warranty questions:

Email: hello@lipa.bio
Subject line: Return request — [Your order number]
Response time: Within 48 hours on business days
